Job Interview Cheat Sheet

Why do you want to work here?

  • Speak to their mission + why you want to support it
  • Outline the advantages the company has relative to competitors
  • Share why you are excited about the immediate role

Tell me about a mistake you made at work

  • Use an example of a real mistake YOU made
  • Use an example where you were able to remedy the mistake - but avoid "catastrophic" mistakes
  • Explain how you fixed the error, what you learned, and add a story about how you avoided a similar situation later in your career

What’re your greatest strengths?

  • Focus specifically on skills that match the role!
  • Provide tangible examples of how those strengths supported previous outcomes (KPIs) in previous roles
  • Provide a mixture of hard & soft skills!

What’s your biggest weakness?

  • Avoid toxicity, anger, or any other “red flag” weakness
  • Talk about a weakness that you’ve overcome through focused effort
  • Explain how you identified and worked through the issue

What’s your plan for your first 90 days here?

  • Get to know the team & its needs
  • Identify and solve a big problem the team wants solved that has no owner
  • Create a plan for the rest of the year that my colleagues and mgr agree with

Tell me about a time you had a disagreement with a colleague or manager

  • Pick an example that ended amicably
  • Start with “in almost all cases at work, everyone has good intent and disagreement comes from lack of shared information”
  • Do not ever denigrate the other party in the story
